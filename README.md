## Potential Content Spoilers

### Ships:

- Soviet Lunar Ship based on the Zond-5 spacecraft (Type A)

- "JubShip" based on the DSS Sauropod from the <a href="https://stevecole.fandom.com/wiki/Astrosaurs">Astrosaurs by Steve Cole</a> (Type A and B)


### Weapons:

- Dung Torpedoes inspired by the <a href="https://stevecole.fandom.com/wiki/Astrosaurs">Astrosaurs by Steve Cole</a> 
        <br/>&emsp;- Description: Rockets laced with dinosaur dung, are the Sauropod's main weapons during space battles. They also appear to be incredibly precise. 
        <br/>&emsp;- Information: Fires up to 3 missiles; does 2 damage each; pierces all shields with a +80% accuracy.


- Dinosaur 'Defence' Artillery 
        <br/>&emsp;- Description: A 'Defensive' Artillery that fires 3 missiles that deal 1 damage. 
        <br/>&emsp;- Information: Fires 3 missiles; 1 damage each; pierces all shields. Can be upgraded to 0.25x cooldown. 


### Augments:

- Dung Magnet inspired by the <a href="https://stevecole.fandom.com/wiki/Astrosaurs">Astrosaurs by Steve Cole</a> 
        <br>&emsp;- Description: Single use combat augment that can be deployed before combat to deal 5 damage to an enemy system of your choice as well as against their hull.

- Green Soup
        <br>&emsp;- Description: Increase all allied crew health by 20% and decrease JubJub's Power Cooldown by 25%


### Drones:

- Sputnik Combat Drone
        <br>&emsp;- Description: An old satellite refitted with a laser attach that will continually attack the enemy ship.


### Races:

- JubJub
        <br>&emsp;- Description: The Dinosaur Ambassador to the Moon (and subsequently the USSR).
        <br>&emsp; - Powers:
            <br>&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;- Clone-Ready: His DNA has been extensively analysed and he can be cloned at 2x speed.
            <br>&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;- Soup: Regains all of his HP upon drinking a speical Soup made by the Soup Dragon.
            <br>&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;- Strong Willed: Somehow JubJub is immune to mind control...
            <br>&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;- Due to his.. keen senses.. JubJub can identify enemy crew even without functioning sesnors.

- Soviet
        <br>&emsp;- Description: A Soviet Cosmonaut from the 20th Century. They thrive on the dismantling of capitalist ideologies and are strongly against private property.
        <br>&emsp;-Powers: Other Soviet crew in the same room gain an additional 25% Max Health and Repair Speed

- Horse
        <br>&emsp;-Description: A highly intelligent and articulate horse.
        <br>&emsp;-Powers:
            <br>&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;- 2.5x move speed
            <br>&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;- 1.5x combat damage
            <br>&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;- Repairs at 0.5x speed
            <br>&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;- Puts out fires at 2.0x speed


- Crab
        <br>&emsp;- Description: A crafty and intelligent species originally from Earth.
        <br>&emsp;- Powers:
            <br>&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;- Divorce: Instantly divorces enemies in the room which stuns them for 5s. Crab cannot move during this time as he is finalising his paperwork. Lock and Load.
            <br>&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;- 0.25x repairs
            <br>&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;- 2x combat damage
            <br>&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;- 1.5x movement speed
            <br>&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;- Heals at double speed


### Sectors:

- Earth 1968
        <br>&emsp;- Description: Time Distorted Sector
        <br>&emsp;- Information: There is something strange about this sector, you feel time slipping away as your approach.
        <br>&emsp;- Quests:
            <br>&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;- 2 quest beacons on arrival, a third available depending on dialogue options. 
            <br>&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;- 2 ship unlocks possible, although not in the same run.
            <br>&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;- 1 drone unlock.
            <br>&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;- Potential for new races to join crew.


### Quests:

- JubJub Quest
        <br>&emsp;- Description: Meet JubJub on the Moon where he currently lives with the Clangers. Learn about his life and help to reshape history.
        
- Soviet Quest
        <br>&emsp;- Description: Help the Soviets get to the Moon to complete their mission.

- Jub/Soviet Quest
        <br>&emsp;- Description: Linking the JubJub Quest and Soviet Quest, additional dialogue options for each and adds a third quest location in the sector.
