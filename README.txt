A small addon for Multiverse 5.5.1 which adds content relating to JubJub.

Content:

- Ships
- Weapons
- Augments
- Drones
- Races
- Sector
- Quests


(Potential Content Spoilers)

Ships:

- Soviet Lunar Ship based on the Zond-5 spacecraft (Type A)

- "JubShip" based on the DSS Sauropod from the Astrosaurs by Steve Cole (Type A and B)


Weapons:

- Dung Torpedoes inspired by the Astrosaurs by Steve Cole
        Description: Rockets laced with dinosaur dung, are the Sauropod's main weapons during space battles. They also appear to be incredibly precise.
        Information: Fires up to 3 missiles; does 2 damage each; pierces all shields with a +80% accuracy.

- Dinosaur 'Defence' Artillery
        Description: A 'Defensive' Artillery that fires 3 missiles that deal 1 damage.
        Information: Fires 3 missiles; 1 damage each; pierces all shields. Can be upgraded to 0.25x cooldown. 


Augments:

- Dung Magnet inspired by the Astrosaurs by Steve Cole
        Description: Single use combat augment that can be deployed before combat to deal 5 damage to an enemy system of your choice as well as against their hull.

- Green Soup
        Description: Increase all allied crew health by 20% and decrease JubJub's Power Cooldown by 25%


Drones:

- Sputnik Combat Drone
        Description: An old satellite refitted with a laser attach that will continually attack the enemy ship.


Races:

- JubJub
        Description: The Dinosaur Ambassador to the Moon (and subsequently the USSR).
        Powers:
            Clone-Ready: His DNA has been extensively analysed and he can be cloned at 2x speed.
            Soup: Regains all of his HP upon drinking a speical Soup made by the Soup Dragon.
            Strong Willed: Somehow JubJub is immune to mind control...
            Due to his.. keen senses.. JubJub can identify enemy crew even without functioning sesnors.

- Soviet
        Description: A Soviet Cosmonaut from the 20th Century. They thrive on the dismantling of capitalist ideologies and are strongly against private property.
        Powers: Other Soviet crew in the same room gain an additional 25% Max Health and Repair Speed

- Horse
        Description: A highly intelligent and articulate horse.
        Powers:
            2.5x move speed
            1.5x combat damage
            Repairs at 0.5x speed
            Puts out fires at 2.0x speed


- Crab
        Description: A crafty and intelligent species originally from Earth.
        Powers:
            Divorce: Instantly divorces enemies in the room which stuns them for 5s. Crab cannot move during this time as he is finalising his paperwork. Lock and Load.
            0.25x repairs
            2x combat damage
            1.5x movement speed
            Heals at double speed


Sectors:

- Earth 1968
        Description: Time Distorted Sector
        Information: There is something strange about this sector, you feel time slipping away as your approach.
        Quests:
            2 quest beacons on arrival, a third available depending on dialogue options. 
            2 ship unlocks possible, although not in the same run.
            1 drone unlock.
            Potential for new races to join crew.


Quests:

- JubJub Quest
        Description: Meet JubJub on the Moon where he currently lives with the Clangers. Learn about his life and help to reshape history.
        
- Soviet Quest
        Description: Help the Soviets get to the Moon to complete their mission.

- Jub/Soviet Quest
        Description: Linking the JubJub Quest and Soviet Quest, additional dialogue options for each and adds a third quest location in the sector.
